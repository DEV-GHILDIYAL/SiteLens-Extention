Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. Please inform us of any allergies or dietary requirements when making your reservation.

What is the dress code for fine dining?

We recommend smart casual attire. Jackets are encouraged but not mandatory.

How far in advance should I book for a private event?

For large events, we recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance. For intimate dinners, 2 weeks is usually sufficient.
